## Theory Application: The Break-Even Bid Framework

From `904_Auction_Draft_Value_Theory.md`, the core question is:

> **At what price does buying a specific player break even vs. the alternative use of those dollars?**

### The Jokic Case Study

**Jokic**: $190 bid, 112.55 FP/G  
**$/FP/G**: $1.69 (highest in league)

According to theory, this is a "Severe Overpay" — but let's examine the roster impact:

- **Budget remaining**: $10 for 9 roster spots
- **Forced to fill with $1 players**: Avg ~40-45 FP/G
- **Team PPG impact**: Jokic's 112.55 + nine 42.5 FP/G players = ~495 total FP/G
- **Alternative**: Spread $200 more evenly — could get 2-3 players at 70-80 FP/G + better depth

**PartyMarty__ (Jokic owner) results**:
- Total Spent: $200
- Avg FPG: 44.73 (11th out of 16 teams)
- Avg $/FP/G: $0.20 (2nd best efficiency)
- **Issue**: Low total FP/G despite efficiency — the "Jokic + trash" problem

**Verdict**: The theory predicted this. Jokic's elite FP/G couldn't overcome roster dilution.

## Actionable Insights for Future Drafts

### 1. The $1-20 Range is Where Championships Are Won
- 92 picks in the $1-10 range averaged 40.22 FP/G
- That's D-tier production for pennies
- **Strategy**: Don't be afraid to let stars go if you can load up on value depth

### 2. Avoid the $76-100 Range
- Avg $/FP/G of $1.23 (worst value tier)
- You're paying A-tier prices for B-tier production
- **Better**: Either go elite ($100+) or drop to $50-75 range

### 3. The Jalen Johnson Lesson
- $40 for 81.75 FP/G (A-tier production at C-tier price)
- Market inefficiency: Breakout candidates are undervalued
- **Strategy**: Target 2nd/3rd year players with expanded roles

### 4. Sabonis Warning
- $90 for 62.84 FP/G is catastrophic
- That's $1.43 per FP/G — you could've bought 3-4 solid players instead
- **Lesson**: Don't overpay for "safe" players who aren't elite

### 5. The "Jesus Christ" Blueprint
- Spend $180-195 (not full $200)
- Avoid any single player over $50
- Target the $20-50 range for your core
- Fill with $1-10 fliers
- **Result**: Highest total FP in the league

## 🎯 The Big Revelation: Availability Changes Everything

When you factor in **games played**, the draft value story completely shifts:

### The Jokic Paradox SOLVED

**Per-Game Value**: Jokic at $190 = $1.69/FP/G (worst in league)  
**Total Season Value**: Jokic delivered 7,316 FP for $190 = **38.5 FP per dollar**

**But wait** — that's still **TERRIBLE** compared to:
- Kevin Porter: $1 for 4,355 FP = **4,355 FP per dollar**
- Donovan Clingan: $1 for 4,734 FP = **4,734 FP per dollar** (2nd year breakout)

**The real issue**: Jokic played 65/80 games (81% availability). He was elite when healthy, but:
1. His $190 cost meant the rest of the roster was $1 players
2. Those $1 players averaged 40-50 FP/G but played 70-80 games
3. **Total roster FP matters more than individual FP/G**

## 🎓 Revised Auction Theory: The Availability Premium

### The New Formula

```
Player Value = (FP/G × Expected GP) / Price
Availability-Adjusted Value = Player Value × (GP / Max Possible GP)
```

## 💡 Actionable Takeaways for Next Year

### 1. The Availability Tax
- **Injury-prone stars**: Discount by 40-50% from "fair value"
- **AD, Giannis, Embiid types**: Max bid should be $40-50, not $90+
- **Sabonis lesson**: Never pay $90 for a guy who might play 20 games

### 2. The Ironman Premium
- Players with 3+ years of 75+ GP history are **undervalued**
- Scottie Barnes, Brunson, KAT types: Worth paying up to $50-60
- They deliver **total season value**, not just per-game spikes

### 3. The $1-20 Sweet Spot
- 92 picks in $1-10 range averaged 40 FP/G × 70 GP = **2,800 total FP**
- That's equivalent to a $40 player who only plays 50 games
- **Strategy**: Load up on 8-10 $1-5 picks, hit on 3-4, win the league

### 4. The "No Man's Land" ($70-110)
- This range is where you overpay for injury risk
- Jokic ($190) is the exception, but even he wasn't optimal
- **Better**: Either go $50-60 for ironmen or $1-30 for value depth

### 5. The Jesus Christ Blueprint (Revised)
- **OLD**: Balanced roster, avoid premiums
- **NEW**: Balanced roster + **prioritize availability**
- Target: 3-4 players at $30-50 (ironmen), fill rest with $1-10 fliers
- Aim for **70+ avg GP** across roster

## 🎯 Final Verdict

**The auction theory from `904_Auction_Draft_Value_Theory.md` was RIGHT** — but it **underestimated the availability factor**.

The real formula isn't just:
> `TeamFP_effective ≈ TeamPPG × MinGames`

It's:
> `TeamFP_effective ≈ (Σ Player_FP/G × Player_GP) / Total_Roster_Spots`

**Games played matters MORE than FP/G ceiling** in total season value.

The teams that won the draft:
1. Hit on $1 picks who played 75+ games
2. Avoided injury-prone stars at premium prices
3. Prioritized availability over per-game upside

The teams that lost:
1. Paid $90+ for players who missed 40+ games (Sabonis, Giannis)
2. Overpaid for "safe" stars who got hurt (AD, Trae)
3. Ignored the availability discount

**Next year's strategy**: Build an ironman roster, not a per-game ceiling roster.
